How to Twyst

  • What is Twyst?

    Twyst is a service that lets you order food from neighbouring restaurants with the convenience of an App. Whats more it lets you earn Cashback everytime you order. The cashback is in the form of ‘Twyst Bucks’ and you can use it to Top Up your mobile phone, get online shopping vouchers on popular eCommerce sites as well as save money the next time you order on Twyst.

  • Why should I use Twyst?

    Twyst not only gives you the convenience of ordering your food through an app, but also lets you earn Cashback every time you order.   • What are Twyst Bucks?

    Twyst Bucks is the currency in which you get your Cashback on Twyst. 1 Twyst Buck = 1 Rupee worth of value.

  • Where can I use Twyst Bucks?

    Twyst Bucks can be used to Top up your mobile phone, get vouchers for online shopping and also vouchers that help you save money when you order on Twyst. We’re working to add more categories where you can use Twyst Bucks. If you would like a particular category or place where you want to be able to use Twyst Bucks, do write to us and we will do our best to bring them on board.

  • I selected Current Location but its showing the wrong address?

    In some cases, the address for a particular GPS location may be incorrectly detected. If you’re sure that the location being detected is correct, you can always edit the actual address before placing the final order.

  • I can’t find XYZ outlet on Twyst?

    We only show you the outlets that deliver to your selected location (you can choose your current location or even a different location) as per the information given to us by the outlet. If you’re sure that the outlet delivers to your area try making sure that your phone is correctly picking up the location by enabling GPS.

  • Can I cancel an order once I have placed it?

    Yes – but only till the restaurant has accepted it. Once they start working on the order, we don’t think it’s fair to allow cancellation. You can however request the outlet and if they’re ok, the order can be cancelled.

  • Why am I charged a ‘Handling Fee’ for some vouchers?

    We charge a small handling fee for some of the vouchers. This helps us pay our bills J and ensures that we keep getting you the Cashback that you love!

  • I ordered on Twyst but I didn’t get any cashback why?

    The only case where you don’t get Cashback for ordering via Twyst is when you apply a food voucher to order on Twyst. You cannot GET Twyst Bucks and USE Twyst Bucks on the same order – but hey, you did save when you applied the offer J. If you didn’t apply an offer to your order but still didn’t get any Twyst Bucks then please write to us and we’ll look into it.

  • Do Twyst Bucks expire?

    Short answer - Yes. Twyst Bucks are valid for a period of 6 months from when they are earned. The Twyst Bucks that you earn earlier will be used earlier every time you redeem, so you shouldn’t have to worry about expiry, but in case you do have expiring Twyst Bucks we’ll notify you well in advance.
